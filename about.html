About Us

Changie Food Center is a family-owned restaurant serving authentic Taiwanese cuisine, cooked with love and passion. Our dishes are homemade with care by Mama and Papa Chang, ensuring quality and a taste of Taiwan in every bite. We use only the freshest ingredients and homemade techniques to create meals that are not only delicious but also comforting and satisfying. Experience the warmth of Changie's ambiance and savor the flavors of our beloved cuisine. You will come to find, that at Changie, Craving never stops!

Changie Food Center: Mission and Vision

At Changie Food Center, our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Taiwan to every table by serving homemade dishes crafted with love, passion, and the freshest ingredients. We are dedicated to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every meal is a comforting experience that satisfies both the soul and the palate. Our vision is to become the go-to destination for authentic Taiwanese cuisine, where each bite creates lasting memories and fosters a deep connection to Taiwan's rich culinary traditions. We aspire to be the place where the craving for quality, homemade food never stops, and Changie is the name that comes to mind.

Taste of Taiwan: The Changie Experience

At Changie Food Center, we proudly cater to a diverse community. Students enjoy our affordable, flavorful meals during lunch breaks or after-school hangouts. Barkadas (groups of friends) find a welcoming spot to share meals and create memories. Local foodies savor our authentic Taiwanese dishes, whether for a quick bite or a full meal. Solo diners appreciate our cozy setting for a convenient and delicious meal. Families value the quality time spent over our shared meals, with options for both adults and children. At Changie, we ensure everyone leaves satisfied and eager to return.

Serving Our Community at Changie Food Center

At Changie Food Center, we are committed to promoting culinary diversity by offering a wide range of dishes that cater to diverse tastes and cravings. We value brand visibility, striving to establish a strong online presence that helps us connect with a broader audience and attract new customers. Boosting sales is another key value, as we encourage both dine-in and takeout options, including group meals with features like "Barkada Cravings." Most importantly, we believe in building community engagement by positioning Changie as a welcoming gathering spot where friends and families can come together to share great food and create lasting memories.
